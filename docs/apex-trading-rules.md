# Apex Trader Funding - Trading Rules Summary

## Overview

Apex Trader Funding is a premier trader funding company specializing in futures market evaluations. The company offers two main account types: **Evaluation (Challenge) Accounts** and **Performance Accounts (PA) / Funded Accounts**. This document provides a comprehensive comparison of all trading rules for both account types.

---

## Key Differences Between Account Types

**Evaluation Accounts** are designed to help traders demonstrate their profitability and trading consistency over a minimum of 7 trading days. These accounts have fewer restrictions and are focused on achieving profit targets while staying within drawdown limits.

**Performance Accounts (PA) / Funded Accounts** are for traders who have passed evaluation and are eligible for payouts. These accounts have stricter consistency rules and risk management requirements to ensure disciplined, sustainable trading.

## Detailed Rule Explanations

### Drawdown Management

**Evaluation Accounts:**
- The trailing drawdown follows the highest peak of unrealized balance in real time
- Drawdown amounts vary by account size (e.g., $1,500 for $25K, $2,500 for $50K, $3,000 for $100K)
- The drawdown stops trailing at the safety net level (initial balance + drawdown + $100)

**PA / Funded Accounts:**
- Trailing drawdown works the same way but stops moving once safety net is reached
- For Static Accounts: Drawdown remains fixed (e.g., $625 for $100K Static account)
- No daily maximum drawdown limit exists for either account type

### Trading Hours and Holiday Rules

**All Account Types:**
- Trading allowed 6 PM ET one day to 4:59 PM ET the next day
- All trades must be closed and pending orders canceled by 4:59 PM ET each day
- Holiday trading permitted if markets are open
- Half-day holidays do not count as trading days; trading is combined with next day
- Trading on Sundays counts as part of Monday's trading day

### Minimum Trading Days

**Evaluation Accounts:**
- Minimum 1 trading day required to pass
- No maximum time limit; traders can take as long as needed

**PA / Funded Accounts:**
- Minimum 10 trading days required for first withdrawal
- Minimum 10 trading days required for all subsequent withdrawals
- Ensures regular trading activity and consistency

### Risk Management Requirements

**Stop Losses:**
- Evaluation: Not enforced
- PA/Funded: Mandatory. Every trade must have pre-defined risk level or mental plan
- For traders on probation: Hard stop-loss levels are mandatory

**5:1 Risk-Reward Ratio:**
- Evaluation: Not enforced
- PA/Funded: Maximum ratio of 5:1. If profit target is 10 ticks, stop loss cannot exceed 50 ticks

**30% Negative P&L Rule (Maximum Adverse Excursion):**
- Evaluation: Not enforced
- PA/Funded: Open unrealized losses cannot exceed 30% of start-of-day profit balance per trade
- For new/low profit accounts: Based on trailing threshold (30% of $2,500 = $750 for $50K account)
- Can increase to 50% if EOD profit doubles the safety net

### Consistency Rules (PA/Funded Accounts Only)

**30% Consistency Rule (Windfall Protection):**
- No single trading day can generate more than 30% of total profit balance at time of payout request
- Resets after each approved payout
- Applies only to profits earned after most recent payout
- Exception: Rule eliminated after 6th payout or transfer to Live Prop Account

**Contract Scaling Rule:**
- Initially restricted to half of maximum allowed contracts
- Full contract limit available once EOD balance exceeds safety net
- Example: $50K account with 10 contract max can trade 5 contracts initially; 10 contracts after reaching $52,600
- Single violation: Close excessive contracts immediately or face payout denial and account reset to previous day's EOD balance
- Repeat violations: Account closure and forfeiture of all balances

**Contract Size Consistency Rule:**
- Must maintain consistent contract approach reflecting disciplined trading
- Scaling adjustments based on account growth acceptable if systematic
- Drastic, inconsistent changes solely to manipulate payouts result in forfeiture and potential probation

### Position Sizing and Hedging Rules

**Max Contracts Rule:**
- Cannot exceed maximum contract limit set for account at any time
- Cannot trade maximum contracts on multiple instruments simultaneously
- Cannot use micro contracts to circumvent max contract rule
- Violations result in account termination without refund

**One-Direction Rule (PA/Funded Only):**
- Can only hold long OR short position at any time
- Strictly prohibited from holding correlated positions simultaneously (e.g., long ES, short YM)
- No hedging across indices, metals, grains, currencies, or correlated instruments

### Trading Strategies

**Flipping Trades:**
- Evaluation: Allowed if generating minimum $50 profit on 5 trading days
- PA/Funded: Allowed but limited to less than 20% of trading days with minimum $50 profit on eligible days

**Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA):**
- Evaluation: Not enforced
- PA/Funded: Allowed with no restrictions on entry points or contract sizes, but must maintain 5:1 risk-reward and other consistency rules

**Adding to Trades:**
- Evaluation: Not enforced
- PA/Funded: Allowed to scale into winning trades if supported by defined strategy and clear directional bias

**News Trading:**
- Evaluation: Allowed during 6 PM ET to 4:59 PM ET window
- PA/Funded: Allowed but cannot take opposing positions on same news event; must maintain consistency with system rules

### Automation and Account Management

**Automation (PA/Funded Accounts Only):**
- Fully automated trading systems strictly prohibited
- AI, autobots, algorithms, and high-frequency trading (HFT) not allowed
- Semi-automated software permitted only if actively monitored by trader
- Set-and-forget or 24-hour continuous trading prohibited

**Account Management (PA/Funded Accounts Only):**
- Must be traded exclusively by registered account owner
- Third-party management, trade copying, and trade mirroring software strictly prohibited
- Violations result in immediate account closure and forfeiture of all funds

### Defined System Requirements (PA/Funded Accounts Only)

**All traders must maintain:**
- Consistent, systematic trading approach with clear rules
- Set guidelines for entries, stops, targets, and trailing stops
- Clear, trackable performance that can be explained if requested
- Predefined directional bias for all trades
- Professional trading discipline and risk management

**Apex may request:**
- Detailed explanation of trading system
- Marked-up charts showing trade execution
- Live Zoom session to observe trading
- Recordings of trading sessions with explanations
- Documentation of tools, indicators, platforms, and contract sizes used

### Prohibited Activities

**Evaluation & PA/Funded Accounts:**
- Trading disallowed instruments (system automatically rejects with no penalty)
- Account sharing, credential sharing, or unauthorized access
- Multiple account creation by same user
- Sharing MAC addresses, computers, IPs, or credit cards

**PA/Funded Accounts (Additional Prohibitions):**
- Using trailing threshold as stop-loss mechanism
- All-in trades with maximum contract sizes for windfall profits
- Cycling through multiple accounts with large sizes then blowing up
- Stockpiling evaluation accounts to blow up in pursuit of luck
- Trading without proper stop losses or risk management
- High-risk strategies (small profit targets with large stop losses)
- High-frequency trading (HFT) or manipulation of simulation environment
- Deviation from professional trading standards

## Payout and Account Management

### Evaluation Account Reset
- Accounts can be reset unlimited times
- Failed accounts that are not reset within 8 days will be disabled if no active subscriptions exist
- Accounts reset to full balance and trailing threshold

### PA / Funded Account Payouts
- Must maintain safety net for first 3 payouts
- Minimum $500 withdrawal requirement
- After 4th payout: Safety net requirement eliminated
- Payouts reset the 30% consistency rule for subsequent profits
- 100% of first $25,000 in earnings; 90% thereafter
- Payouts typically processed same day

### Account Probation
- Placed when specific compliance guidelines need attention
- Provides opportunity to improve and restore account to good standing
- Hard stop-loss orders become mandatory during probation
- Failure to meet probation requirements results in denial of payouts, profit removal, or account closure
